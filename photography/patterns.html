PIECE IN A JIGSAW PUZZLE

Everything that surrounds us is part of something bigger. The wall is part of the apartment. The apartment is part of the house. The house is part of the estate. The estate is a part of the city. Even our planet is part of the solar system, that is part of a galaxy, that is part of a galaxy cluster, that is part of the universe, that it is ...possibly part of something even bigger. Who knows. It is important to be able to find and appreciate beauty in even the smallest and simplest things. The beauty of color, texture, structure, symmetry or the lack thereof. The more we appreciate the little things, the more we can appreciate the bigger ones.
