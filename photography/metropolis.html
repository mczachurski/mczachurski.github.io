METRÓPOLIS

Several thousand years ago, mankind underwent a revolution known as the Neolithic revolution. Nomadic lifestyle has been changed into a sedentary one. The first cities and civilizations arose. It was the great cities that began to mean more and more in the history of mankind. Here the most eminent scholars, poets and philosophers gathered. In them, successive revolutions - scientific, economic or political - emerged. Many of the first cities did not survive to our times, but they showed the way. The path that humanity is still following.
