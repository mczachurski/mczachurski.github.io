TRANSFER OF MATTER OR ENERGY

Fast transport is something we cannot imagine our life without. Thanks to him we can work farther from home, buy things from the farthest places in the world that can reach us in a few days. We can travel and visit the farthest corners of the world during even a few days' holidays. We can organize cultural, sports, political and many other events and invite people from all over the world. It is thanks to fast transport that the world has become much smaller. Fast transport, the history of which is no longer than one century, has revolutionized our lives. It is hard to imagine what our life would be like if transport to any place were instantaneous. Who knows, maybe we'll find out one day...
