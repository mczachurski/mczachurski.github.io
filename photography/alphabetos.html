ALPHABĒTOS

The history of the alphabet and writing as such is several thousand years old. Writing is one of the most important, if not the most important, invention of mankind. It is difficult to imagine the development of our entire civilization without it. We get to know the alphabet and writing as children and it accompanies us throughout our lives. Signs of various alphabets can be found in various places of the reality that surrounds us. Very often they are not visible at first glance.
