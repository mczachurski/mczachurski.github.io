UN POUR TOUS

We live in a completely divided world. Half of the population hates the other half. Nationalists hate liberals. Believers hate unbelievers. Vaccines hate anti-vaccines. Blue people hate green. Square people hate round ones. All because of the message coming to us from the media such as TV, radio, newspapers, social networks. Because of the bubbles in which social networks close us. A message created by a handful of people overwhelmed by the possession of power. They don't know yet that they are going to fail because people have more love, compassion and willing to learn than hate. At least I hope so.
