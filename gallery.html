PHOTOGRAPHY

Looking at a photo, we are able to move our thoughts to a completely different time and place in an instant. It can be a different city, country or continent. An event captured moments ago or several decades ago. We are able to stand next to the people in the photo, smile at them, greet them and warn them against danger. We can get angry, cry, or get excited. We can sense the emotions of the people captured in the photo, their thoughts. Their respect, love, anger or contempt. So many emotions, feelings or memories locked in one small piece of paper. Photography is a journey in time and space. Photography is magic.
